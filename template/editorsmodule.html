Editors’ picks

Notable fiction books of 2015

Crime, history, escape, romance, the supernatural — from novelists both new and seasoned.

Notable nonfiction books of 2015

“Aurora,” “The Fifth Season,” “Three Moments of an Explosion” and more.

Best romance novels of 2015

Books by Sonali Dev, Joanna Shupe, Kate Meader, Alisha Rai and Emma Chase are standouts.

Best poetry collections of 2015

Works by Tony Hoagland, Yusef Komunyakaa, Rowan Ricardo Phillips, Amy Gerstler and Juan Felipe Herrera.

Best mystery books and thrillers of 2015

Suspenseful works from Elizabeth George, Sara Paretsky, Ruth Rendell, John Grisham and Richard Price.

Best science fiction and fantasy books of 2015

“Aurora,” “The Fifth Season,” “Three Moments of an Explosion” and more.

Best audiobooks of 2015

Among the top five are “The Tsar of Love and Techno” and a collection of short stories in "Honeydew: Stories.”

Best graphic novels of 2015

Among the top five are the second volume of a civil rights icon’s journey and a revisiting of “Sandman.”
